About Us

Learn about our mission, vision, and the team behind Climate Action Now

Our Mission

Climate Action Now is dedicated to mobilizing communities worldwide to take meaningful action against climate change through education, advocacy, and sustainable practices.

Our Vision

We envision a world where humanity lives in harmony with nature, where clean energy powers our societies, and where climate justice ensures a sustainable future for all.

Our Story

Climate Action Now was founded in 2015 by a group of environmental scientists and community activists who recognized the urgent need for grassroots climate action.

What began as a small local initiative has grown into a global movement with chapters in over 25 countries. Our organization brings together people from all walks of life who share a common concern for our planet's future.

Over the past decade, we've planted over 15,000 trees, implemented 50+ community-based climate projects, and educated thousands of people about climate science and sustainable practices.

Our Approach

Education

We provide accurate, accessible information about climate science and solutions to empower informed decision-making.

Advocacy

We advocate for climate-friendly policies at local, national, and international levels to drive systemic change.

Direct Action

We implement practical climate solutions through community projects, from reforestation to renewable energy initiatives.

Collaboration

We partner with businesses, governments, and other NGOs to maximize our collective impact on climate change.
