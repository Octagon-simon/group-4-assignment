Ways to Take Action

Everyone has a role to play in addressing climate change. Find the path that works best for you.

Volunteer With Us

Join our global network of volunteers making a tangible difference in communities worldwide. Whether you have a few hours or a few days, your time and skills can help advance climate solutions.

Current Volunteer Opportunities:

  • Community tree planting events
  • Beach and river cleanups
  • Climate education workshops
  • Renewable energy installations
  • Administrative and technical support
Sign Up to Volunteer

Support Our Work

Your financial contribution directly funds climate action projects around the world. We ensure that every dollar is used effectively to create maximum impact.

Your Donation Impact:

  • $25 plants 10 trees in deforested areas
  • $50 provides climate education for 20 students
  • $100 helps install solar panels in underserved communities
  • $500 funds a community climate resilience project
Make a One-Time Donation Become a Monthly Supporter

Advocate for Change

Use your voice to influence climate policy at local, national, and international levels. Our advocacy toolkit provides resources to help you effectively communicate with decision-makers.

Advocacy Actions:

  • Contact your elected representatives
  • Sign and share our climate petitions
  • Participate in peaceful climate demonstrations
  • Engage with businesses about their climate practices
  • Join our Rapid Response Network for urgent advocacy needs
Download Advocacy Toolkit

Spread Climate Awareness

Education is a powerful tool in addressing climate change. Help spread accurate information about climate science and solutions in your community.

Educational Resources:

  • Host a climate conversation with our discussion guide
  • Share our social media content with your network
  • Organize a climate film screening or book club
  • Bring our climate curriculum to local schools
  • Become a certified Climate Ambassador
Access Educational Materials
